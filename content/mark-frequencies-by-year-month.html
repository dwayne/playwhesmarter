Mark frequencies by year/month

(Click here to skip to the frequencies)

Find out how many times a mark has played for a given year. That means, if you want to know how many times a mark has played in 2018, 2017, 2016, all the way down to the year Play Whe started, 1994, then this is the tool you use.

You can also find out how many times a mark has played for a given month in a given year. That means, if you want to know how many times a mark has played in January 2018, February 2017, March 2016, all the way down to July 1994, then again this is the tool you use.

You'd notice that the frequencies are broken down by the time of day for your convenience.

We hope you can use this data to find some interesting marks to play. Have fun and may the mark be with you.
