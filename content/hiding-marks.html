Hiding marks

(Click here to skip to the tables)

Find out which marks haven't played in a long time. We call these marks hiding marks. And, we say that they are hidden.

Hiding marks have a high chance of playing in the next draw, so run them down.

How to find hiding marks?

  1. Firstly, sort all the Play Whe results for the last month or two by descending order of date (i.e. most recent date first).
  2. Write them down on a piece of paper.
  3. Go through the list from top to bottom taking note of ONLY the first time you see a given mark in the list. That's the last time the mark played.
  4. Repeat the previous step for every mark. Since there are 36 marks you've got to do it 36 times.
  5. Finally, the last mark you find is the mark that hasn't played in the longest time. That's your TOP hiding mark and a mark you should probably run down.
